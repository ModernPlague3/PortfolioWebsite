Ad Astra Avionics

Ad Astra Avionics is a forward-thinking startup dedicated to revolutionizing avionics for rocketry and high-performance engineering. Our plug-and-play systems integrate state-of-the-art hardware and software, delivering real-time telemetry and robust power management to ensure optimal performance. Designed for rocketry teams, hobbyists, and technical clubs—including organizations like Baja Racing—our mission is to empower innovators with reliable, scalable solutions that push the boundaries of aerospace.

B.R.U.N.O Avionics computer

B.R.U.N.O Avionics computer is engineered as the central nervous system for advanced aerospace vehicles. With its compact 5x3 inch form factor, dual-microcontroller architecture, high I/O capability, and integrated GPS/LoRa communications, B.R.U.N.O delivers precise real-time data and control for flight-critical applications. Our mission is to provide a robust, scalable platform that meets the rigorous demands of both commercial and experimental flight.

J.A.X.O.N motor and servo AI Driver

J.A.X.O.N motor and servo AI Driver is an ultra-low cost, high-performance control system leveraging the ESP32 to deliver precise motor and servo control. Utilizing encoder data and powered by a USB-C PD IC for streamlined power management, J.A.X.O.N offers versatile connectivity via serial and I2C—enabling seamless integration with edge AI modules like the Jetson Nano. Built from readily available modules, this innovative solution combines autonomous control capabilities with advanced communication for cutting-edge applications.
